I was staying in McKinney Falls just outside Austin, but wasn't able to keep my spot through the holidays. Luckily I was able to find another campsite about 30 miles West of Austin in Dripping Springs.

This is where we ended up spending Christmas. My buddy Patrick was also in town so we spent Christmas Eve playing board games at his place.
